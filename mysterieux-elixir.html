Mystérieux Elixir

An aromatic masterpiece crafted for the discerning gentleman. A captivating symphony of nutmeg, cinnamon, cardamom and invigorating grapefruit upon first spritz.

The base notes of liquorice, sandalwood, amber, patchouli, vetiver impart depth and sophistication.

Evoking a timeless sense of elegance and masculinity, it combines spicy, citrusy top notes with a warm, woody foundation, creating an olfactory journey that is both refined and irresistibly alluring.
